Typography Powers the Web: The Hidden Art Behind Readability

From pixels to paragraphs — why font styling matters more than ever
@RoxyRock

Typography is more than just choosing a pretty font — it’s what makes the difference between a reader staying or bouncing away. As users scroll through endless content, text that’s well-aligned, well-spaced, and beautifully styled can grab attention like a headline in ink.

In this digital age, CSS gives designers the power to fine-tune readability like never before. Properties such as line-height, text-indent, and font-weight may seem small, but combined, they build rhythm and hierarchy. The web might be built on code, but good design? That’s built on type.
